Sure. Here is another example of how EDD can be used in C#. Suppose we have a class called Product and a class called Customer. We want the Product to be able to send an event to the Customer when the product is purchased.

We can do this by creating an event called Purchased in the Product class. We can then add a handler to the Purchased event in the Customer class. When the Product is purchased, the Purchased event will be raised. The handler in the Customer class will then be called.
